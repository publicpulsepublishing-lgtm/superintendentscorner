How It Works

Advertising That Comes With a Built-In Endorsement

Our model is different from a typical media buy. When a school district is featured in Superintendent's Corner, district leadership provides a list of their key suppliers. Those suppliers receive a personal invitation from the district to place an ad in the edition.

That means your ad doesn't just appear next to relevant content. It appears because the district you already work with invited you to be there. That is a level of credibility that no banner ad or cold email can match.
